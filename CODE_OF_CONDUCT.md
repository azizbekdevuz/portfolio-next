# Code of conduct

## Our standards

Participants in this project (issues, pull requests, discussions) are expected to:

- **Be respectful** of others’ time, background, and opinions.
- **Stay on topic**; keep technical discussion factual and professional.
- **Not** use sexualized language or imagery, attacks, trolling, harassment, or doxxing.
- **Not** make sustained disruption of issues or review threads.

## Project scope

This is a **personal portfolio** repository. The owner may **decline** contributions, close issues, or lock threads that are off-topic, spam, or in conflict with the goals of the project.

## Reporting

Unacceptable behavior may be reported to the repository owner at **azizbek.dev.uz@gmail.com**. The owner will consider context and use proportionate action (e.g. warning, temporary block, or removal from the project) when behavior violates these rules.
